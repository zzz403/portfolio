## Overview

An interactive visual novel that teaches forensic science concepts through hands-on investigation, evidence analysis, and deductive reasoning.

## Key Features

- **Branching narrative** — player choices affect case outcomes and story progression
- **Evidence collection** — examine crime scenes with interactive tools and techniques
- **Forensic puzzles** — DNA analysis, fingerprint matching, toxicology reports
- **Educational integration** — real forensic science concepts woven into gameplay
