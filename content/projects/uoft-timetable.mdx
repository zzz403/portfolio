## Overview

A comprehensive timetable planning tool for University of Toronto students with 2,000+ active users.

## Key Features

- **Course search** with real-time filtering across all UofT campuses
- **Conflict detection** that highlights overlapping sections instantly
- **Schedule optimization** to minimize gaps and commute time
- **Export** to Google Calendar, iCal, and image formats
