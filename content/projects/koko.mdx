## Overview

KOKO is a voice-first social platform built to make digital communication accessible and warm for older adults. Co-founded and developed from the ground up.

## Key Features

- **Voice-first interface** — no typing required, fully tap-less interaction
- **Community circles** — connect seniors within neighborhoods and interest groups
- **Cross-community bridges** — facilitate inter-generational and cross-community connections
- **WebRTC-powered** real-time voice with low latency and high reliability
